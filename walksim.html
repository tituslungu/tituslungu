Walking Simulator Mechanism

Cleveland State University

Designed a mechanism to simulate human walking motion. Mechanism desigend for kinematics course with the scope of being used as a shoe testing platform. The paper below, Walking Simulator Mechanism, has been published in "The Downtown Review" at Cleveland State University. The CAD models used in the project are provided for further development and research here. Please use the suggested citation in the paper below to cite the original project in any subsequent work.
