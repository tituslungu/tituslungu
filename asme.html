Hexapod Robotics Project (2013-2014)

As president of ASME, I led a team of about ten engineering students in the design and early stages of prototype construction of a hexapod using 3D printing, manual lathes, CNC manufacturing, and Arduino. This six legged robot was created for salting snowy walkways and spreading fertilizer on various terrains.
